About

A founder building for the digital economy.

Silvanus Nzubechi Sunday is the founder and Chief Executive Officer of Yong Social, a Technology, Information & Media company building products that inform, empower, and create opportunities in the global digital economy.

He founded Yong Social with a mission to make information, finance, and wealth-building simple, honest, and actionable for everyone — regardless of background or experience.

His work spans technology, digital media, product development, growth, financial education, and community building, with a focus on creating durable digital products that compound in value over time.
